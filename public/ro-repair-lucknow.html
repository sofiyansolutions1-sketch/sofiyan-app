1. How often should I get my RO water purifier serviced?

We recommend a basic service every 3 to 6 months, and replacing the RO membrane and filters every 12 to 18 months, depending on the TDS levels in your area.

A bitter or metallic taste usually means the RO membrane has ruptured or the post-carbon filter is exhausted and needs replacement.
