When Does Your Split AC Need Service?

Reduced Cooling

If your AC is running but not cooling properly, it may need filter cleaning, gas refilling, coil cleaning, or compressor inspection.

Water Leakage

Blocked drain pipes, dirty indoor units, or installation issues can cause water dripping.

Bad Smell or Noise

Dust buildup, mould, loose parts, or fan issues may lead to unpleasant smell and unusual sound.

Higher Electricity Bills

An unserviced AC can consume more power. Regular maintenance helps improve efficiency and reduce electricity usage.

Seasonal Maintenance

We recommend pre-summer AC servicing in February and March for better cooling performance during peak heat.
