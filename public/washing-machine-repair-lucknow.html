Common Problems We Fix

  • Not Spinning: Drive belt issues, worn-out motor, or broken lid switch.

  • Not Draining: Clogged drain hose, faulty drain pump, or blocked filter.

  • Leaking Water: Damaged hoses, faulty water inlet valves, or worn door seals.

  • Excessive Noise or Vibration: Unbalanced load, worn drum bearings, or broken shock absorbers.

  • Not Starting at all: Main PCB board fault, timer issue, or faulty power cord.
